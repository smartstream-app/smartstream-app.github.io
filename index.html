Transform your IPTV subscription into a high-end, AI-powered streaming library. Whether you connect via Xtream Codes or M3U, this app organizes your movies and series into a beautiful, metadata-rich interface.

🎥 Intelligent Library Management

Stop scrolling through endless lists. Our engine automatically categorizes your content and pulls high-quality metadata from TMDB.

  • Missing Data? Integrated AI fills the gaps, ensuring every title has a description, poster, and cast info.
  • Multilingual Support: Fetch metadata in your preferred language automatically.
  • Smart Filtering: Sort by genre, language, year, rating, or alphabetical order.

🧠 AI-Powered Discovery

Your library learns from you. By providing your own Google Gemini API key, the app analyzes your ratings to suggest your next favorite binge-watch. It’s a truly personalized recommendation engine fueled by your unique tastes.

🏠 Whole-Home Sync & Quick Install

Experience seamless transitions between rooms. Start a movie in the living room and pick up exactly where you left off in the bedroom.

  • Cloud Backup: Connect your Google Drive to instantly sync preferences, ratings, and metadata to any new TV in your home. No more tedious setups.

🔒 Privacy & Security First

We believe your viewing habits are your business.

  • Full Encryption: Data is protected via HTTPS in transit and AES-256 at rest.
  • User-Controlled Keys: Secure your library with a personal PIN.
  • Discreet Content: Adult content is automatically moved to a secure XXX folder with full metadata support.
