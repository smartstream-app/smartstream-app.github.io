Transform your IPTV subscription into a high-end, AI-powered streaming library. Connect securely via Xtream Codes to organize your movies and series into a beautiful, metadata-rich interface.

🎥 Intelligent Library Management

Stop scrolling through endless lists. Our engine automatically categorizes your movies and series and pulls high-quality metadata from TMDB.

  • Missing Data? Integrated AI fills the gaps, ensuring every title has a description, poster, and cast info.
  • Multilingual Support: Fetch metadata in your preferred language automatically.

🧠 AI-Powered Discovery

Your library learns from you. By providing your own Google Gemini API key, the app analyzes your ratings to suggest your next favorite binge-watch.

🏠 Whole-Home Sync

Experience seamless transitions between rooms. Connect your Google Drive to instantly sync preferences, ratings, and metadata to any new TV in your home.

🔒 Privacy & Security First

We believe your viewing habits are your business.

  • Full Encryption: Data is protected via HTTPS in transit and AES-256 at rest.
  • User-Controlled Keys: Secure your library with a personal PIN.
  • Discreet Content: Adult content is automatically moved to a secure XXX folder with full metadata support.
