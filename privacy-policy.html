Privacy Policy for SmartStream

Last Updated: March 2026

Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the SmartStream application ("the App") handles your data.

1. Google Drive Data (OAuth)

SmartStream uses the Google Drive API (`https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.file` scope) exclusively to backup and restore your personal app settings, watch history, and profiles. We do not have access to your Google Drive. The App can only see and modify the specific backup files that the App itself creates.

2. Data Storage & Sharing

All data is processed and encrypted locally on your device before being synced to your own personal Google Drive account. We do not operate external servers, we do not collect telemetry, and we absolutely do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.

3. Your Consent

By using the Cloud Sync feature in SmartStream, you consent to the app communicating directly with your Google account strictly for the purpose of backing up your personal configuration.
