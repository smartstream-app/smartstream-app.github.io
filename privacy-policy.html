Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Data Collection & Control

Our application is designed as a "Bring Your Own Data" (BYOD) utility. We do not host, store, or see your media content.

  • User-Provided Keys: You provide your own Google Gemini API and Google Drive credentials. This data is stored locally on your device and encrypted.
  • No Third-Party Tracking: We do not sell your viewing habits to advertisers. Your ratings and watch history stay within your private Google Cloud environment.

2. Encryption and Security

  • In-Transit: All communication between the app and external APIs (TMDB, Google Cloud, IPTV Providers) is forced via HTTPS (TLS 1.3).
  • At-Rest: Your configuration files and PIN-protected folders are encrypted using AES-256 bit encryption.

3. Third-Party Services

By using the AI recommendation and cloud sync features, you interact with:

  • Google Gemini API: To generate metadata and recommendations.
  • Google Drive: To store your personal backup and sync files.
  • TMDB: To fetch movie and series posters and descriptions.

Please refer to these providers' respective privacy policies for how they handle data on their end.
